About



Claude is an AI tool that helps in writing, summarizing, and understanding long texts.

Features



  • Summarizes long content
  • Helps in writing
  • Understands detailed information
  • Gives clear explanations

Uses



It is useful for students and writers who work with long documents and need clear summaries.
